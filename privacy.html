Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 14, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Mini OpenStax. We are committed to protecting the privacy and security of our learners, educators, and visitors. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our educational platform, which is proudly aligned with the NaCCA standards-based curriculum for Ghanaian learners.

Our core philosophy is privacy-by-design. We believe that students should be able to learn, track their progress, and take quizzes without worrying about their personal data being harvested or sold.

2. Information We Collect

Mini OpenStax is designed to be a privacy-first, offline-capable educational tool. We do not require account creation, and we do not collect personal data on central servers.

  • Local Profile Data: When you use the settings menu (the gear icon) to enter your name and select a role (Student or Instructor), this information is saved only in your browser's local storage.
  • Learning Progress: Your quiz scores, attempt history, and mastery levels are stored locally to help you track your progress on the Dashboard and Analytics tabs.
Note: Because your data is stored locally on your device, it will not be visible if you switch to a different device, use a different browser, or clear your browser's cache and site data.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information stored locally is used exclusively to enhance your learning experience:

  • Personalizing your dashboard with your name.
  • Displaying your quiz history, correct/incorrect breakdowns, and performance metrics.
  • Enabling the "Instructor" view for educators to see simulated class analytics and BI charts.
  • Shuffling quiz options using a seeded RNG to ensure fair and consistent assessments.

4. WhatsApp Sharing

Our platform includes a feature to share quiz results via WhatsApp. When you click the "Share" button after a quiz:

  • We use the official WhatsApp URL scheme (wa.me) to open the app or web interface.
  • A pre-formatted text message containing your name, quiz score, and date is generated.
  • You have full control to edit, delete, or cancel the message before sending it to any contact.

We do not have access to the messages you send, your WhatsApp contacts, or your chat history.

5. Third-Party Services & External Links

To provide a rich, modern learning experience, our lessons may include connections to external resources:

  • YouTube: Embedded or linked documentaries (e.g., inauguration ceremonies).
  • Google Fonts: We load the Inter and Playfair Display typefaces to ensure beautiful, readable typography.
  • Font Awesome: For the icons used throughout the interface.

These third-party services have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them, particularly Google's privacy policy regarding font loading.

6. Children's Privacy

Mini OpenStax is primarily designed for Basic School learners (Class 6 and above) and secondary students. We do not knowingly collect any personally identifiable information on our servers. Since all data is kept locally on the user's device via localStorage, we inherently comply with international and Ghanaian children's data protection standards.

7. Data Security

Since your data never leaves your device, it is protected by your device's own security measures and browser sandboxing. We recommend keeping your device locked, using a secure browser, and regularly updating your software to ensure maximum security.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our platform, new features, or updates to Ghanaian data protection regulations (such as the Data Protection Act, 2012 (Act 843)). Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
